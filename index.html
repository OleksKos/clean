FAQ

Common questions about our cleaning services answered.

Do I need to be home?
You don't need to be present, but we recommend being available for the first visit. We'll call ahead and confirm arrival times. Access to your property is all we need to complete the work safely.
Why are windows left wet?
We use purified water and water-fed poles for streak-free results. Windows dry clear without spotting naturally. This method is gentler on frames and leaves no residue behind.
How often should I clean?
We recommend gutter cleaning twice yearly (Spring/Autumn) and window cleaning every 4-8 weeks. Driveway cleaning depends on usage and weather, but usually once a year is sufficient.
Are frames and sills included?
Yes, we clean frames and sills as part of our window service. Gutters are fully cleared including downpipes. All exterior surfaces are treated as part of the job description.
